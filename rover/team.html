Meet Team QBOTIX

A passionate group of innovators, engineers, and dreamers from Kumaraguru College of Technology.

Forging the Future of Space Robotics

Team QBOTIX is a multidisciplinary student team from Kumaraguru College of Technology, united by a passion for robotics and space exploration. We are dedicated to designing and building a next-generation Mars rover to compete in the International Rover Challenge 2026.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of autonomous systems, develop innovative solutions for extraterrestrial exploration, and inspire the next generation of engineers.
